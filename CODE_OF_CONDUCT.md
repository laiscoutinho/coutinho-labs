# Coutinho Labs — Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful and inclusive  
- Accepting constructive feedback  
- Communicating clearly and professionally  

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or offensive language  
- Discrimination of any kind  
- Personal attacks or trolling  

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to:

contact@coutinholabs.com

All reports will be reviewed and handled appropriately.

---

## Responsibility

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code and ensuring a healthy environment for all contributors.

---

This Code of Conduct exists to ensure a professional and collaborative space.
